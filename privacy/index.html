Privacy Policy

K12 Tools is built for schools and does not collect, sell, or share student data. We keep this policy short and readable on purpose.

Privacy promises

  • No advertising
  • No selling data
  • No tracking for targeted ads
  • No student accounts
  • No collection of student personal information
  • No cookies

Local storage only

Some tools store settings (like student lists or preferences) in the browser using localStorage. This data stays on the device and never leaves the browser.

Standard security logs

Our hosting provider may keep standard security logs to protect the service. These logs are not used to identify individual users.
