Privacy Policy

K12 Tools is built for schools and does not collect, sell, or share student data. We keep this policy short and readable on purpose.

Privacy promises

  • No advertising
  • No selling or sharing data
  • No tracking for targeted ads
  • No student accounts
  • No collection of student personal information
  • No cookies
  • This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which does not use cookies, does not track individual users, and does not collect personal or student data.

Local storage only

Some tools store settings (such as student lists or preferences) in the browser using localStorage. This data stays on the device and never leaves the browser.

Standard security logs

Our hosting provider may maintain standard security logs to protect the service. These logs are not used to identify individual users.
